If you're setting up DStv Stream on a Smart TV or compatible streaming device, you may be asked to visit **dstv.stream/tv** and enter a unique TV activation code. This secure activation process links your television to your DStv Stream account, allowing you to stream live TV, sports, movies, series, and other available content.

This guide explains how to activate DStv Stream, enter your TV code, troubleshoot common activation issues, and securely access your streaming account.

Activate Your DStv Stream Device
--------------------------------

Follow these simple steps:

#. Open the **DStv Stream** app on your Smart TV or streaming device.
#. Select **Sign In**.
#. A unique TV activation code will appear on your screen.
#. On your phone, tablet, or computer, visit **dstv.stream/tv**.
#. Sign in to your DStv Stream account if prompted.
#. Enter the TV activation code exactly as shown.
#. Click **Activate** or **Continue**.
#. Wait a few moments while your device is connected.
#. Your DStv Stream home screen will load automatically.

Supported Devices
-----------------

You can activate DStv Stream on supported devices such as:

* Smart TVs
* Android TV
* Google TV
* Apple TV
* Streaming media players
* Compatible set-top boxes

Device availability may vary depending on your region.

TV Code Not Working?
--------------------

If your activation code doesn't work, try the following:

* Enter the code exactly as displayed.
* Make sure the activation code hasn't expired.
* Generate a new code from the DStv Stream app.
* Verify your internet connection.
* Sign in using the correct DStv Stream account.
* Refresh the activation page and try again.

DStv Stream Sign-In Problems
----------------------------

If you can't access your account:

* Verify your email address and password.
* Reset your password if you've forgotten it.
* Update the DStv Stream app.
* Restart your TV or streaming device.
* Check whether DStv Stream services are temporarily unavailable.

Tips for Secure Activation
--------------------------

* Keep your device connected to a stable internet connection.
* Use the latest version of the DStv Stream app.
* Enter your activation code before it expires.
* Do not share your account credentials.
* Sign out from shared or public devices after use.

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

**Do I need to enter a TV code every time?**

No. After your device has been successfully activated, you'll usually remain signed in unless you sign out or reset the app.

**Can I activate DStv Stream on multiple devices?**

Yes. Depending on your subscription and account limits, you may be able to activate multiple compatible devices.

**Why did my activation code expire?**

Activation codes are temporary for security purposes. Simply generate a new code from the DStv Stream app and repeat the activation process.

Conclusion
----------

Using **dstv.stream/tv** is the easiest way to activate DStv Stream on a supported Smart TV or streaming device. After entering your TV code and signing in to your account, your device will be securely connected, allowing you to start streaming your favorite entertainment within minutes.
